Dr. Sunday Onimisi Salami

Consultant Orthopaedic / Trauma / Arthroscopist

He is a consultant orthopaedic surgeon with special interest in arthroscopy and treatment of sports injuries. He has more than a decade of experience in medical practice. Dr Salami has undergone extensive training in arthroscopic surgeries in India and is now focusing on treatment of conditions of the knee,shoulder and ankle by key hole surgical techniques. His interests include meniscal surgery, knee ligament reconstruction, cartilage injury, shoulder instability and general orthopaedics. He is a fellow of the West African College of Surgeons and member Nigerian Orthopaedic Association(NOA); International Society of Arthroscopy, Knee surgery and Orthopaedic Sports medicine(ISAKOS);European Society for Sports Traumatology, Knee Surgery and Arthroscopy(ESSKA).
