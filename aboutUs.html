About Us

Grandville Medical and Laser Clinic is an international based skin, body, health and beauty care hub, headquartered in Surulere, Lagos, Nigeria. Established June 1st 2013, we specialize in a range of body, skin care and health treatment of some which include but are not limited to abdominal fat removal, permanent tattoo removal, face lift, ear reconstruction, breast lump, and breast cancer treatment. This is a full Aesthetic Clinic with services involving both invasive and non invasive treatment.


We also do full medical and surgical care with both in-patient and out-patient services. Emergency services are available with consultant review at point of entry.

Our Invasive treatments involve the surgical care ranging from Plastic Surgery, Orthopedic Surgery and so on. The non invasive procedures are dermal filling, Botox injections for fine lines and wrinkles.

We also do full laser treatments for skin tags, facial rejuvenation, permanent hair reduction, hair regrowth in alopecia. We focus on a safe world class procedure in order to achieve a vivacious state of health in our client at reasonable price.

Popular Procedures

Abdominal Fat Removal

Improper fat distribution easily becomes a source of growing concern for many people, more so in Africa where the average fat distribution is more to the arms, the belly and the thigh. Our radio-frequency machine does well in body fat removal through a non-surgically technique and it is very safe for use in all age.

Stretch Mark Treatment

Stretch marks are usually caused by a break in the dermal (inner) layer of the skin by the underlying fat. The dermis represents 95% of the total skin volume. What we do at Grandville is regeneration of the skin and reduction of the underlying fats as a main method of treatment.

Facelift

While coloured skin has been noticed to age gracefully because of its high melanin content, the pigment responsible for protecting us from the ageing effect of the sun. Asides the sun, other factors responsible for skin aging include individual genetic constitution, disease, nutrition e.t.c. Over the years many methods have been observed to slow down ageing from use of organic products like Argan oil, facial chemical peeling, laser peeling, low level laser and radio-frequency – all which are available at Grandville.

Scar Revision

Scars can be very disfiguring especially when they are on obvious areas of the body. Scars which lie across natural skin tension line are obvious scars visible from afar. In scar revision, the plastic surgeon rearranges the scar to fall long the natural skin tension line to make it oblivious to an observer. At Grandville, we offer great scar revision techniques which will make those scars appear less obvious compared to their natural original appearances.

Arthritis Pain Treatment

We offer the use of low level laser in the treatment of arthritic pain. This is an advanced method with many benefits to those who suffer from arthritic pains.

Body Firming

We ensure a smoother and firmer skin tissue at Grandville through the use of radio-frequency devices in our body firming procedure. This device helps in dermal restructuring and body fat reduction consequently giving you a firm and smooth skin.
