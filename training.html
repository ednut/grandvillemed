Skin Treatment Training
Rationale: A good skin treatment limits skin irregularities, such as blemishes or acne scars and guarantees sustained youthful skin.
Training Objective
To ensure better understanding of skin sensibility, functionality and provide vital knowledge on skin treatment using basic laser system and authentic skin care products.
Training Content
The course content include the following:
  • Skin Physiology.
  • Pathology of common Skin Diseases
  • Hair Physiology
  • Basic Laser System Treatment – (including types and mechanism of operation).
  • Chemical Peeling – (Including types and physiology of chemical peeling).
  • Micro Needling.
  • How to start and manage a Laser Clinic.
Course Benefits
At the end of the training participants will be able to manage the following skin conditions:
  • Permanent Hair Removal.
  • Skin Rejuvenation.
  • Skin Tags removal.
  • Moles Removal.
  • Tattoo Removal
  • Vein Removal
  • Keloid/Hypertrophic Scar Treatment.
  • Sunburn Treatment
  • Body polish
  • Wrinkles treatment
  • Acne Treatment
  • Chemical Peeling
  • Stretch Mark Treatment
  • Cellulite Treatment
Target Audience
  • Physicians.
  • Beautician.
  • Nurses
  • Professionals with good knowledge of science.
Course Delivery Mode
  • One on one training .
  • Discussion.
  • Audio – Visual.
  • Intensive practical exercise.
  • Module book on all treatments.
  • Use of ND:YAG laser, Cold Laser and Radio-frequency device
Course Duration
Advanced professional: 2 weeks
Basic Professional: 4 weeks
Course Certification
At the end of the course, participants will be engaged in an open book examination on all topics treated and only candidates who score 75% and above will qualify for certification at the end of the course
